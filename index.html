Hi, I'm Alice.

About

I'm passionate about the intersection of product innovation and ethical technology. I believe we can use technology to create a responsible digital future and a more inclusive and compassionate world.

Currently I lead generative AI product engineering at Pinterest. I coach and unlock high-performing engineering teams to deliver impactful products through rapid prototyping. I'm also a builder specializing in product backend and iOS.

Projects

Compassionate Search

Led engineering for a product that connects Pinterest users showing signs of mental health distress with vital mental health resources. Featured in the New York Times' Good Tech Awards for making positive societal impact.

Pinterest TV

Launched Pinterest's first-ever livestreaming and live shopping product. Developed from zero-to-one within TwoTwenty, Pinterest's in-house new products incubator.

Sprout (in beta)

Driven by my personal experiences (and several close calls) with my tree nut anaphylaxis, Sprout is an app that connects food allergy sufferers with gold-standard resources and restaurant reviews to help everyone feel safer on their path to food allergy freedom. Reach out for a beta invite.

Moments (A Photo Gratitude Journal)

Created Moments, a social and visual gratitude journal app. Our lives have become dominated by technology, and we can forget to pause and enjoy the moment. Technology should serve as a bridge, not obstacle, to humanity. By giving users an avenue to commemorate and share their positive moments with close ones, I hope that Moments will help people remember to appreciate the small things.

AccessiApp

AccessiApp is an Android app prototype that improves the mobile experience for visually impaired users through haptic technology.

Community involvement

compileHer (formerly UChicago FEMMES)

Founded an organization that introduces middle-school girls in South Side Chicago to computer science through hands-on activities and education to inspire the next generation of tech enthusiasts.

Google Women in Engineering Mentorship Program

Launched a global mentorship program for women engineers and scaled this to 6 countries with 100+ participants. I was invited to present to Google's director of global diversity.

William H. Ray Elemantary Coding Club

Teamed up with a non-profit advisor to teach fourth- and fifth-graders from diverse socioeconomic backgrounds intro to coding skills using Processing.js.
